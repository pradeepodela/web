Using Custom Nouns

Custom nouns are a great way of making sure that you generate the right username each and every time. A custom noun could be your name, a place you've recently been to, or anything else that qualifies as a noun that's personal to you. What is a prefix?

A prefix is something appended at the front of your newly generated username. You can choose from verbs, adjectives, or entering your own username prefix. That way the pseudonym or nickname you end up generating always starts with the right type of word. Go ahead, try it!
